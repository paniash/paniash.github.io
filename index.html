Hello there!

My name is Ashish Panigrahi. I'm a 3rd year physics major studying under the Integrated Master's programme at the National Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhubaneswar, India. My interests lie in the realm of quantum physics with its application in algorithms and error-mitigation for NISQ devices and beyond.

I like to learn and tinker with software and occasionally write code (in Python and C) during my leisure hours. I am a strong proponent of GNU/Linux, using free (as in freedom), open-source and privacy-respecting software over proprietary ones by Microsoft, Google and other tech giants.

If you have any questions, suggestions, advice or simply wish to discuss about topics that relate to me, feel free to contact me via email.
